Proudly Family Owned And Operated

Our family run bakery started in 1923 with a family, baking bread for their friends. Over the years we have grown leaving the home kitchens and working in a state-of-the-art small bakery. We may have started with bread, but our menu quickly expanded into cakes, pies, and cupcakes with more coming soon. We may be a small-town baking company, but we serve customers all over Eau Claire County and even beyond. We specialize is complex cake designs for any occasion, we also make numerus kinds of other baked goods. We gladly attend numerus farmers markets and other community events.
